Ingredients

  • 3 bananas, overripe
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of baking soda
  • dash of salt
  • 1 egg (real or flax egg)
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 4 tbsp chocolate chips or M&M's
  • (Optional) 1/2 cup of sourdough starter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Mash bananas in a large bowl
  3. Put flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, egg, and starter in the bowl
  4. Melt butter and add it to the bowl, stirring until just blended
  5. Pour into a greased load pan
  6. Sprinkle on chocolate chips
  7. Bake for 70 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean
